# TikTok Account Warmup

Sources: @rossark0 (May 2026), @alexcooldev (May 2026), @lucaspatiri_ (May 2026)

## 5-Day Warmup Process

### Days 1-2: Become a Viewer, Not a Creator

**DO NOT post anything for the first 48 hours.**

Open the app and behave like your target user. You are training the algorithm in real time.

**Daily checklist:**
- [ ] Scrolled the feed for 10 minutes, twice a day
- [ ] Liked 20-30 videos from your niche
- [ ] Watched 10 videos until the very end, no skipping
- [ ] Left 5 genuine comments (no emojis, no one-word replies)
- [ ] Followed up to 5 relevant accounts
- [ ] Saved 10 videos as format references
- [ ] Tapped "not interested" aggressively on off-niche content

**Goal by end of Day 2:** Your For You page shows exactly the type of content you want to make. If it doesn't, you moved too fast.

**Warmup killers:**
- Watching a video for 3 seconds and scrolling away (negative signal)
- Following 50+ accounts on day one (red flag)
- Posting before 48 hours (wasted effort)

### Day 3: Test Before You Commit

Post 2 slideshows first (low-effort diagnostic tool).

**KPIs within 5 hours of posting:**
- 500+ impressions (minimum)
- 20-30+ likes (mandatory)

If you hit those numbers: post up to 3 of your main videos that same day.
If you don't: keep warming up. One more day of observer behavior > a wasted video on a cold account.

### Day 4+: Acceleration

- Post 2 videos per day
- Keep interacting with the feed for 10 minutes, twice a day
- Like, save, and comment on content from your niche
- Watch which videos perform best and adjust format accordingly
- Do NOT stop the daily routine just because you're posting

## Account Setup Rules

- **Use an existing email** for new accounts. Brand new emails get shadowbanned.
- **Username must look normal** ("justinworksout" not "fitapp_promo_23").
- **Bio:** only tag the main app account. No promotional language.

## Warmup Is Daily, Not One-Time

If you stop behaving like a human on the app (stop watching, liking, commenting), the account loses its signal and TikTok stops trusting it. Even a viral account dies if you stop the daily viewer behavior. Treat it like brushing your teeth.

## Retention: The Only Metric That Matters

Target 42%+ watch-through to the end. Below this threshold, distribution waves stop early.

**Video structure for retention:**
- First 3 seconds: Hook. Must interrupt the scroll.
- Middle: Move fast enough that dropping off feels like a loss.
- Last 3 seconds: Earn the watch. If the ending trails off, cut it.

## When to Abandon an Account

1. 6 consecutive videos stay under 300 views
2. Try posting 3 videos recorded natively inside TikTok (no third-party edits, no CapCut). Sometimes this resets the account's trust signal.
3. If still no reaction: abandon and start a new account
4. The warmup takes 2 days. Starting over costs 2 days. That is not a loss. Staying on a dead account for 3 weeks IS.
