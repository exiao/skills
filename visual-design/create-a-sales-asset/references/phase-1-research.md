# Phase 1: Research

### Assess Context Richness

| Level | Indicators | Research Depth |
|-------|------------|----------------|
| **Rich** | Transcripts, detailed pain points | Light: fill gaps only |
| **Moderate** | Some context, no transcripts | Medium: company + industry |
| **Sparse** | Just company name | Deep: full research pass |

### Tooling

Use available tools:
- **serper-search**: Google search for prospect info, news, financials
- **web_fetch**: Pull prospect websites, press releases, investor pages
- **browser**: Deep-dive prospect product pages, careers

### Always Research

1. **Prospect basics**: `"[Company]" annual report investor presentation 2025 2026` — revenue, employees, key metrics, strategic priorities
2. **Leadership**: `"[Company]" CEO CTO CIO 2025` — names, titles, recent quotes
3. **Brand colors**: `"[Company]" brand guidelines` or extract from website

### If Moderate/Sparse Context

4. **Industry context**: `"[Industry]" trends challenges 2025 2026`
5. **Technology landscape**: `"[Company]" technology stack tools platforms`
6. **Competitive context**: `"[Company]" vs [seller's competitors]`

### If Transcripts Uploaded

7. **Conversation analysis**: extract stated pain points, decision criteria, objections, timeline, key quotes (use their exact language), terminology, internal project names
