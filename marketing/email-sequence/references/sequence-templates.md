# Email Sequence Templates

Detailed templates for common email sequences.

## Contents
- Welcome Sequence (Post-Signup)
- Re-Engagement Sequence
- Onboarding Sequence (New Subscribers)
- Win-Back Sequence (Expired Trials)

## Welcome Sequence (Post-Signup)

**Email 1: Welcome (Immediate)**
- Subject: Welcome to [Product] — here's your first step
- Deliver core value proposition
- Single next action (e.g., add your first stock)
- Set expectations for future emails

**Email 2: Quick Win (Day 1-2)**
- Subject: Get your first [result] in 10 minutes
- Enable small success
- Build confidence
- Link to helpful resource or deep-link into the app

**Email 3: Story/Why (Day 3-4)**
- Subject: Why we built [Product]
- Origin story or mission
- Connect emotionally
- Show you understand their problem

**Email 4: Social Proof (Day 5-6)**
- Subject: How [User] achieved [Result]
- User story or testimonial
- Relatable to their situation
- Soft CTA to explore

**Email 5: Overcome Objection (Day 7-8)**
- Subject: "I don't have time for investing research" — sound familiar?
- Address common hesitation
- Reframe the obstacle
- Show easy path forward

**Email 6: Core Feature (Day 9-11)**
- Subject: Have you tried [Feature] yet?
- Highlight underused capability
- Show clear benefit
- Direct CTA to try it (deep-link)

**Email 7: Conversion (Day 12-14)**
- Subject: Ready to unlock everything?
- Summarize value
- Clear offer
- Urgency if appropriate
- Risk reversal (free trial, money-back)

## Re-Engagement Sequence

**Email 1: Check-In (Day 14-30 of inactivity)**
- Subject: Is everything okay, [Name]?
- Genuine concern
- Ask what happened
- Easy win to re-engage (deep-link to a feature)

**Email 2: Value Reminder (Day 2-3 after)**
- Subject: Here's what you've been missing
- Remind of past value
- What's new since they left
- Quick CTA

**Email 3: Incentive (Day 5-7 after)**
- Subject: We'd love to have you back
- Offer if appropriate
- Limited time
- Clear CTA

**Email 4: Last Chance (Day 10-14 after)**
- Subject: Should we stop emailing you?
- Honest and direct
- One-click to stay or go
- Clean the list if no response

## Onboarding Sequence (New Subscribers)

Coordinate with in-app onboarding. Email supports, doesn't duplicate.

**Email 1: Welcome + What's Unlocked (Immediate)**
- Confirm subscription
- Highlight what's now available
- Deep-link directly to premium features

**Email 2: Getting Started Help (Day 1-2)**
- If they haven't explored premium features
- Quick tip or walkthrough
- Support option

**Email 3: Feature Highlight (Day 3-5)**
- Key premium feature they should know
- Specific use case
- Deep-link into the app

**Email 4: Success Story (Day 5-7)**
- User who got value from premium
- Relatable journey
- Motivational

**Email 5: Check-In (Day 14)**
- How's it going?
- Ask for feedback
- Offer help
- Soft ask for App Store review if engaged

## Win-Back Sequence (Expired Trials)

**Email 1: Trial Ended (Day 1 post-expiry)**
- Subject: Your trial ended — here's what you're missing
- Recap what they used during trial
- Show what's locked now
- Clear CTA to subscribe

**Email 2: Feedback Request (Day 7)**
- Subject: Quick question about your experience
- What held you back?
- Short survey or single question
- Use responses to segment follow-ups

**Email 3: Incentive (Day 14)**
- Subject: A special offer, just for you
- Discount or extended trial
- Limited time
- Clear CTA

**Email 4: Final Reach-Out (Day 30)**
- Subject: The door's still open
- Low pressure
- Mention improvements since they left
- Easy path back
