# Remix Strategy (Alex Ruber)

- Search your niche keywords on X, LinkedIn, Substack
- Find top 50 performing hooks from the last 30 days
- Remix each hook 3x with your angle
- 95% of content fails - every win comes from tweaking hooks that already worked
- Don't try to be fully original. The sauce is remixing what works.

## Viral shell transplant

Source: Mino Lee, `@minolee.mp4`, Instagram Reel `DVOp94KD3dz`, Feb 2026.

Use this when the user needs a fast hook for short-form video and has no taste-data yet.

1. Find an outlier in the same content format, not just the same niche. Minimum bar: 100K+ views, or clearly above account baseline.
2. Copy the first sentence exactly as a structure. Treat it like a shell, not like prose.
3. Change only 2-3 nouns/keywords to your niche. Preserve the syntax, promise shape, and simplicity level.
4. Repeat the transplant for each beat of the script, not just the opening line.
5. Use minimum viable editing: phone camera, captions, one-line text chunks, fast cuts. Do not let production quality become the bottleneck.
6. Post before over-polishing. The test is whether the shell carries your idea.

Example shell:
- Original: `My trading strategy explained so simply that a 5-year-old could understand`
- Transplant: `Creating viral IG Reels explained so simply that a 5-year-old could understand`
- Bloom: `AI stock research explained so simply that a 5-year-old could understand`

Why it works: the viral asset is the sentence architecture. The niche swap keeps the proven curiosity, accessibility, and cadence while making it relevant.
