# Audience Segments

**Purpose:** Map audience segments to pains, desired outcomes, objections, and messaging angles before writing ads or organic content.

Use this as a template. Replace bracketed placeholders with the category, product, and market you are working on.

---

## Quick Segment Matrix

| Segment | Current Pain | Desired Outcome | Messaging Angle |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| New-to-Category | Knows something is wrong but lacks vocabulary | Understand the problem and first step | "Finally, a simple way to solve [pain]" |
| Budget-Conscious | Wants progress without large upfront cost | Lower-risk path to value | "Start small, prove value, then scale" |
| Burned-Out Existing User | Tried alternatives and got frustrated | Relief, clarity, fewer tradeoffs | "A workflow that actually fits your life" |
| Power User | Already has a workaround | More speed, control, or leverage | "Upgrade the system you already built" |
| Time-Constrained Buyer | Wants outcome but lacks time | Done-for-you or easier setup | "Get the result without adding another chore" |
| Skeptical Buyer | Has been burned before | Proof, transparency, guarantees | "See exactly how it works before committing" |

## Segment Template

### [Segment Name]

**Who They Are:**
- [Observable identity or situation]
- [Trigger event that makes the problem urgent]
- [What they have already tried]

**Pain Points:**
- [Pain in the customer's own words]
- [Operational or emotional cost]
- [Why existing options feel inadequate]

**Desired Outcome:**
- [Functional result]
- [Emotional result]
- [Status or identity shift]

**Key Objections:**
- "[Why now?]"
- "[Why this product?]"
- "[Why should I trust it?]"

**Messaging Angles:**
- Before/after transformation
- Effort reduction
- Risk reversal
- Proof and specificity

**Formats to Test:**
- Problem confession
- Comparison card
- Founder/talking-head explanation
- Testimonial or case-study snippet
- Short product demo

---

## Usage

1. Pick 3-5 segments before generating creative.
2. Pull exact language from reviews, comments, support tickets, sales calls, or Reddit threads.
3. Write one angle per segment and awareness level.
4. Test segment-specific hooks instead of one generic campaign message.
