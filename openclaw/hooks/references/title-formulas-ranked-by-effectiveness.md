# Title Formulas (Ranked by Effectiveness)

### Tier 1 — Highest Click-Through
1. **"I [did specific thing]. [Unexpected result]."**
   - "I deleted half my AI's prompt. It got smarter."
   - "I texted my AI three words. It rewrote its own brain."

2. **"[Number] [things] [unexpected outcome]."**
   - "99 failures in 8 categories. One agent fixed them all."
   - "363 tests to see if AI can pick stocks."

3. **"[Contrarian claim that creates tension]."**
   - "Most AI stock tools just Google things for you."
   - "Every AI assistant is trapped in a browser tab."

### Tier 2 — Strong
4. **"[Situation]. [But/twist]. [Result]."**
   - "Production broke at 11pm. I fixed it from bed. Never opened my laptop."

5. **"How [unexpected method] [achieves desired outcome]"**
   - "How 3 text messages replaced a week of prompt engineering."

6. **"Why [thing everyone does] [doesn't work / is wrong]"**
   - "Why 95% of AI prompts have instructions the model doesn't need."

### Tier 3 — Use Sparingly
7. **"The [framework/method] for [outcome]"** — only if the framework name is genuinely novel
8. **"X things I learned [doing Y]"** — listicle energy, works but generic
9. **"How to [do thing]"** — SEO friendly but low curiosity
